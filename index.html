Intro

I'm a second year PhD student at the University of St Andrews in the Schools of Physics and Astronomy and Earth and Environmental Sciences. My research is focusing on the effect of lighting on the atmospheric chemistry of Earth and other planets and investigating its impact on the origin of life. This includes Miller-Urey like sparking experiments and simulations of chemical processes in planetary atmospheres.
