Hi there, I'm Patrick Barth!

I'm a PhD student in Astronomy and Geology at the University of St Andrews and part of the Centre for Exoplanet Science. I'm working on the effect of lighting on the atmospheric chemistry of Earth and other planets and investigating its impact on the origin of life. This includes Miller-Urey like sparking experiments and simulations of chemical processes in planetary atmospheres.
